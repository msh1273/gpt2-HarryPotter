HarryPotter FanFiction


Introduce

This is a pre-trained GPT-2 generative text model that allows you to generate your own Harry Potter fanfiction, trained off of the top 100 rated fanficition stories.

How to use

  1. Enter a keyword or sentence in the input box below and click the Start Story button. ex)Harry loves Herimone
  2. A Harry Potter story starting with the entered keyword or sentence is created.
  3. The length limit for generated sentences is between 150 and 200 characters.
  4. Click the More Story button to check more stories around the created story.
